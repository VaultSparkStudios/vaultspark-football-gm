A Franchise That Has
Real Stakes

VaultSpark is built around the idea that franchise simulation should feel like running an actual organization — every decision with lasting consequences.

🌍

World-State Engine

Owner personality, staff culture, scheme identity, and player agent personalities all affect outcomes — not just ratings.

📊

PFR-Calibrated Stats

Season and career statistics are guardrailed against real NFL baselines from Pro Football Reference. Numbers feel real.

🔥

Rivalry DNA

Teams build genuine rivalry heat over time based on matchup history, division battles, and playoff upsets. It escalates.

🏆

GM Legacy Arc

Your career is tracked across every season. Progress through 6 tiers — from Young Gun to Immortal — based on what you actually build.

Beat Reporter + Narratives

Weekly milestones, press conference quotes, and franchise story events surface automatically. Your simulation tells its own story.

Local saves, IndexedDB (250MB), and GitHub Gist cloud sync. Rewind to any of the last 10 major decisions. Nothing gets lost.

Choose your team's weekly tactical focus — run-heavy, pass-heavy, blitz package, or prevent defense — before each simulation advances.

Veteran Mentorship

Veterans accelerate younger players' development during training camp. Managing your roster's age curve becomes a strategic tool.

🎨

Franchise Brand Builder

Name your team, customize your city, set your colors. Your franchise, your identity — custom name and color theme applied throughout.
