About Franchise Architect

Franchise Architect is the VaultSpark Studios sports simulation universe for players who want to build organizations, not just manage lineups.

The first release is Franchise Architect: Football, a browser-native franchise game focused on roster construction, contracts, scouting, draft strategy, rivalries, league history, and long-save consequences.

The shared architecture is designed so future sports can live under the same universe with sport-specific rules and shared simulation, save, draft, history, and user-interface packages.
